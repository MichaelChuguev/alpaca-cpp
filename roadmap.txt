1. WebSocket Streaming

Account updates
Trade updates

Missing:
Bracket orders (OCO - One Cancels Other)
Trailing stop orders with offset
Advanced time-in-force options (OPG, CLS, etc.)

Missing:
Detailed portfolio analytics
Historical performance metrics

Missing:
Dividend tracking

Missing:

Options chains

Missing:
